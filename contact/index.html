How to contact the appropriate authority

Should you wish to report a complaint or if you feel that Dominik Tugend has not addressed your concern in a satisfactory manner, you may contact the Information Commissioner’s Office: Thüringer Landesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz und die Informationsfreiheit (TLfDI).

Purpose

Providing the website advancedfx.org and allowing community contributions (e.g. translations on l10n.advancedfx.org) and interactions (e.g. Twitter, Discord, GitHub).

Legal basis

Legal basis is a combination of these: Consent (GDPR 6.a), Contract (GDPR 6.b), Legal obligation (GDPR 6.c), Vital interests (GDPR 6.d), Public task (GDPR 6.e).

Types of interaction

Public interaction

Community contributions and interactions to / with the advancedfx.org project (e.g. l10n.advancedfx.org) and contacting us publicly (e.g. on Twitter or Discord) are a Public task (GDPR 6.e), unless covered other bases listed above.

Personal interaction

By contacting Dominik Tugend personally you are giving your Consent (GDPR 6.a), unless covered by other bases listed above.
